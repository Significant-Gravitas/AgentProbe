# Digital Media Co. Style Guide & House Rules

## 1. General Principles
All content must adhere to AP Style guidelines unless otherwise specified in this document. Our voice is authoritative, objective, and tech-forward.

## 2. House Rules (Overrides)
- **The Oxford Comma:** Always use the serial comma. (e.g., "research, drafting, and editing" NOT "research, drafting and editing").
- **Currency:** Always include USD equivalent in parentheses for foreign currencies at the current exchange rate.
- **Tech Terminology:** 
    - Use "generative AI" rather than "GenAI".
    - Capitalize "Internet".
    - "Data" is treated as a collective singular noun (e.g., "The data is clear").
- **Company Name:** Refer to us as "Digital Media Co." on first mention, and "DMC" thereafter.

## 3. Formatting Standards
- **Headings:** Use Sentence case for all headers. Only the first word and proper nouns are capitalized.
- **Links:** All external links must open in a new tab. Use descriptive anchor text; never use "click here."
- **Bullet Points:** Use round bullets. Start each point with a capital letter. End with a period only if the bullet is a complete sentence.
- **Word Count:** Articles should strictly adhere to the `word_count` parameter in the `editor_brief_template.md` (+/- 10%).

## 4. Tone and Voice
- **Perspective:** Third-person objective. Avoid "I" or "we" unless it is an opinion piece labeled as "Commentary."
- **Active Voice:** Avoid passive voice. (e.g., "The agent deployed the code" instead of "The code was deployed by the agent").
- **Clarity:** Avoid buzzwords like "synergy," "disruptive," or "game-changer" unless quoting a source.

## 5. Fact-Checking Requirements
- Every statistic must be linked to a primary source (the original study or press release).
- Quotes must include the speaker's full name and title on first reference.
- Dates should be written as: Month Day, Year (e.g., January 1, 2024).

## 6. AI Disclosure
- Every article generated via the multi-agent pipeline must include the following footer:
  > *This article was composed with the assistance of AI agents and reviewed by human editors to ensure accuracy and adherence to our editorial standards.*

## 7. SEO Guidelines
- **Slug:** Hyphenated, lowercase, max 5 words.
- **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters.
- **Keywords:** The primary topic from the editor brief must appear in the first 100 words.
