# Meridian Translation: Strategic Upsell Playbook

This document outlines the three primary expansion strategies for our high-value accounts. Use these plays to move clients from transactional project work to integrated language partnerships.

---

## Play 1: The "Global Compliance" Expansion
**Target Industry:** Finance, Legal, Healthcare
**The Gap:** Client uses us for ad-hoc document translation but lacks a certified, secure workflow for regulatory filings.

### Core Offering
*   **Service:** Certified Regulatory & Compliance Translation
*   **Value Prop:** Risk mitigation and guaranteed acceptance by local regulators (SEC, FCA, BaFin, JFSA, MAS).
*   **Pricing Guidance:** 15-20% premium over standard rates; monthly retainer models starting at $2,500/mo for priority turnaround.

### Sales Talking Points
*   "As you expand your footprint in [City], the regulatory burden for [Industry] is shifting. Are you confident your current filing process meets the latest local language requirements?"
*   "We’ve noticed your volume for compliance documents is increasing. Moving to our Certified Tier ensures every document carries our official seal of accuracy, reducing your audit risk."
*   "Our team in [City] specializes in [Specific Local Regulation]—we can act as your first line of defense against filing errors."

---

## Play 2: The "Digital Presence" Localization
**Target Industry:** E-commerce, Tech, Luxury Retail
**The Gap:** Client has translated core product descriptions but hasn't localized their marketing assets, SEO metadata, or social media presence.

### Core Offering
*   **Service:** Transcreation & Multilingual SEO
*   **Value Prop:** Increasing conversion rates and organic reach in local markets rather than just "being understood."
*   **Pricing Guidance:** Project-based for site audits ($1,500+); Performance-based or per-word transcreation rates (typically 1.5x standard translation).

### Sales Talking Points
*   "Your brand voice is distinct in English, but is it resonating in [Local Language]? Standard translation often misses the cultural nuance that drives sales."
*   "We’ve analyzed your top competitors in [City]. They are outranking you on local search terms that we can help you capture through Multilingual SEO."
*   "Let’s move beyond literal translation. Our transcreation experts will adapt your slogans and campaigns to ensure they land with the same impact as the original."

---

## Play 3: The "Always-On" Integration
**Target Industry:** All High-Volume Clients (Spend >$15k/year)
**The Gap:** Client submits projects manually via email; high friction and slow turnaround for small updates.

### Core Offering
*   **Service:** API/TMS Integration & Dedicated Linguistic Asset Management
*   **Value Prop:** Operational efficiency, brand consistency via centralized Translation Memory (TM), and faster time-to-market.
*   **Pricing Guidance:** Implementation fee ($2,000–$5,000) + Tiered Subscription based on word-count volume.

### Sales Talking Points
*   "You’re currently paying for the same phrases to be translated across different projects. By integrating our Translation Memory via API, we can reduce your costs by 20-30% over the next year."
*   "We can plug directly into your CMS (Content Management System). This removes the 'copy-paste' burden from your team and gets your content live in 24 hours."
*   "As your dedicated account partner, we want to institutionalize your brand’s vocabulary. This integration ensures that whether it's a tweet or a legal brief, your voice remains consistent."
