# Loomstep Brand & Voice Guidelines

## 1. Core Identity
Loomstep provides high-precision evaluation infrastructure for production LLM systems. We speak as engineers to engineers. Our brand is defined by technical authority, pragmatism, and the elimination of "vibe-based" engineering.

## 2. Voice and Tone
*   **Confident but Grounded:** We lead the conversation on LLM observability because we build the tooling. We do not need to boast; the technical depth of our content should demonstrate our expertise.
*   **Technical but Accessible:** Use industry-standard terminology (e.g., "RAG," "latency p99," "token usage," "ground truth") without unnecessary jargon. Explain complex concepts through the lens of utility.
*   **Active Voice:** Always favor active constructions. (e.g., "Loomstep automates regression testing" instead of "Regression testing is automated by Loomstep.")

## 3. Style Constraints
*   **No Superlatives:** Avoid words like "revolutionary," "game-changing," "unparalleled," or "best." Let the metrics and the methodology speak for themselves.
*   **Concrete Metrics:** Whenever possible, use specific numbers. Use "Reduce hallucination rates by 40%" rather than "Significantly improve accuracy."
*   **Directness:** Get to the point quickly. Our target audience (Heads of AI and Senior Engineers) is time-constrained and values density of information.

## 4. Visual & Formatting Standards
*   **Typography:** Clean, monospaced fonts for code snippets; sans-serif for body copy.
*   **Structure:** Use clear hierarchies with H1, H2, and H3 headers. Use bulleted lists for technical features or benefits to ensure scannability.
*   **Call to Action (CTA):** CTAs should be imperative and specific. (e.g., "Register for the Technical Deep Dive" instead of "Click Here").

## 5. Audience Alignment: Series B+ SaaS
Our ICP is focused on scale, reliability, and ROI.
*   **Focus on:** Production stability, regression prevention, cost optimization, and developer velocity.
*   **Avoid:** "Hello World" examples or surface-level AI hype. We address the "Day 2" problems of LLM deployment.

## 6. Prohibited Phrases
*   "The future of AI is here."
*   "Unlock the power of LLMs."
*   "Seamless integration" (Use "Two-line SDK integration" instead).
*   "Magic" or "Black box."
