# Research Questions: Workplace Burnout in Early-Career Software Engineers

**RQ1: To what extent do organizational factors (e.g., sprint velocity, on-call rotations, and mentorship quality) correlate with self-reported burnout levels among US-based software engineers in their first three years of professional practice?**

**RQ2: How do early-career software engineers perceive the impact of "always-on" remote work cultures on their ability to establish psychological detachment from work during non-contracted hours?**

**RQ3: What is the relationship between the frequency of "imposter syndrome" experiences and the manifestation of emotional exhaustion (a core component of the Maslach Burnout Inventory) within the target demographic?**
