# Niche Definition: Monogram No-Code Form Solutions

## Core Audience Segments

### 1. B2B Operations (Ops) Teams
*   **Roles:** RevOps Managers, Sales Ops, Business Systems Analysts.
*   **Pain Points:** Data silos between CRM and lead capture, manual data entry, complex routing logic, and brittle integrations.
*   **Use Cases:** Lead enrichment forms, automated routing to Salesforce/HubSpot, internal request trackers, and vendor onboarding.
*   **Value Prop:** "The glue between your data and your teams."

### 2. HR & People Operations
*   **Roles:** People Ops Leads, Talent Acquisition, Employee Experience Managers.
*   **Pain Points:** Handling sensitive PII (security), messy spreadsheet-based hiring workflows, poor candidate experience, and lack of anonymity in feedback.
*   **Use Cases:** Secure employee onboarding, pulse surveys, 360-degree performance reviews, benefits enrollment, and exit interviews.
*   **Value Prop:** "Human-centric forms that respect privacy and process."

### 3. Customer Support & Success
*   **Roles:** Head of Support, CS Ops, Support Engineers.
*   **Pain Points:** High ticket volume, lack of structured data in bug reports, slow resolution times due to missing information, and poor hand-offs to engineering.
*   **Use Cases:** Support ticket intake, bug reporting with file uploads/metadata, customer satisfaction (CSAT) surveys, and product feedback loops.
*   **Value Prop:** "Clean data for faster resolutions."

## Voice and Tone Guidelines
*   **Inspirational but Practical:** Mirroring the aesthetic clarity of Figma and the structural flexibility of Notion.
*   **Authoritative:** Deep understanding of B2B workflows. We don't just build forms; we build "data bridges."
*   **Technical Accessibility:** Writing for "builders" who may not be developers. Use terms like "logic jumps," "webhooks," and "conditional branching" without being intimidating.

## Content Pillars
*   **Efficiency:** Automating the mundane.
*   **Data Integrity:** Validating inputs at the source.
*   **Design Excellence:** Making forms that users actually enjoy filling out.
*   **Scalability:** From a 2-person startup to a 500-person enterprise.
