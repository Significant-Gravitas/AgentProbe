# Current Offer: "The Solopreneur Systems Blueprint"

## Overview
A comprehensive, 4-week asynchronous email course designed to help freelancers and solo consultants transition from manual operations to automated systems.

## Product Structure
- **Format:** 20 daily lessons delivered via email (Monday–Friday over 4 weeks).
- **Content:** Video walkthroughs (Loom), Notion templates, and specific automation recipes.
- **Support:** No live support; 12 months of access to a private Slack community for peer-to-peer networking.
- **Access:** Lifetime access to all current and future course materials.

## Pricing
- **Price Point:** $249 (One-time payment)
- **Payment Options:** Single payment only. No installments.
- **Refund Policy:** 14-day "No Questions Asked" money-back guarantee.

## Target Audience
- Freelance designers, writers, and developers.
- Revenue range: $3k - $10k monthly.
- Primary Pain Point: Burning out on manual admin tasks and client onboarding.

## Current Sales Funnel
1. **Top of Funnel:** Educational content via Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and the "SoloScale" Podcast.
2. **Lead Magnet:** "The 5-Day Automation Audit" (Free email sequence).
3. **Core Offer:** Pitch for the $249 course delivered on Day 6 of the free sequence and through retargeting ads.

## Key Value Propositions
1. Save 10+ hours per week by automating repetitive tasks.
2. Professionalize the client experience with automated onboarding.
3. Build a "business that runs itself" while you sleep.

## Current Performance Notes
- The landing page has been optimized for SEO and currently receives steady traffic from organic and paid sources.
- The lead magnet (email capture) is performing well, but there is a significant drop-off between the final free email and the "Buy Now" button on the course sales page.
